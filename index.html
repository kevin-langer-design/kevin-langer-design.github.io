Applying the Right Strategy

Keeping the user in mind I advocate crafting strategies to identify and eliminate pain points and increase intuitive use. Iterating on projects and continuing to problem solve to bring about solutions are some of the best spells in my designer spellbook. Communicating time, technical restraints, forecasted troubles, and being prepared ensures a project is well managed and set up for success.

Working with Teams

I love working with teams and learning from those who give knowledge and provide some of my own in return. The best work comes when minds are put together. Being a team player means more than just doing your part in the project, it also means you're putting your finger on the pulse of the project and everyone involved. Identifying points where the team needs to pivot or adapt is crucial for success. We're all stronger when we work together.
